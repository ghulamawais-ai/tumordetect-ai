About the Project

AI-Powered Brain Tumor Diagnosis

Automating early detection using state-of-the-art deep learning and explainable AI.

Objective

Assist radiologists by automating brain tumor detection from MRI scans, delivering pixel-level segmentation, multi-class classification, and transparent AI explanations — all in one unified system.

Core Technologies

Dense Attention U-Net for segmentation · ResNet50 (fine-tuned) for 4-class tumor classification · Grad-CAM for explainability · CLAHE + RGB/HSV/LAB fusion for preprocessing · ReportLab for PDF generation.

Clinical Impact

Transparent AI diagnosis with Grad-CAM visual explanations empowers clinicians to validate predictions, understand what the model sees, and make confident, well-informed decisions for patient care.

Under The Hood

Two AI Models Working Together

Each model plays a distinct role in the diagnostic pipeline.

Segmentation

Dense Attention U-Net

Pixel-level tumor boundary detection

  • Dense skip connections for rich feature reuse
  • Attention gates suppress irrelevant brain regions
  • Outputs binary tumor mask at full resolution
  • Measures tumor width, height, area in cm
  • Morphological post-processing to clean noise
Classification

ResNet50 (Fine-Tuned)

4-class tumor type identification

  • Pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on MRI data
  • Layers 3 & 4 + FC head unfrozen for training
  • Class-weighted loss for imbalanced data
  • ~80% test accuracy across 4 tumor classes
  • Grad-CAM taps layer4 for explainability
